6/55 Grand Lotto

2D Lotto

Pick 2 numbers from 1 to 31. Choose 'S' for straight or 'exact order', 'R' for rumble or 'any order', and 'P' for pompi or 'same number'. Match the drawn numbers and win! It's simple and exciting—give it a try today!

3D Lotto
